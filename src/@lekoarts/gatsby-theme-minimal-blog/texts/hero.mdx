If you are reading this perhaps you are like me, a technologist disillusioned and unsatisfied by the practice of software development in industry. 
The daily dysfunction of building commercial software is far from the engineering discipline I imagined it to be. 
Though I've spent a great deal of time thinking about this problem, pure contemplation has accomplished nothing productive. 
**How a love for programming is lost and how to get it back**, is the motivating question. The following inquiry is an earnest attempt to develop an answer.
